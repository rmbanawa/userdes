Persona

Activity#6


Personas are fictional characters, which you create based upon your research in order to represent the different user types that might use your service, product, site, or brand in a similar way. Creating personas will help you to understand your users’ needs, experiences, behaviours and goals. Creating personas can help you step out of yourself. It can help you to recognise that different people have different needs and expectations, and it can also help you to identify with the user you’re designing for.

Some of our group persona below:

Lebron is a sales representative in a company, doing tasks that require visual concentration on the computer screen in order not to have errors on his input. He doesn't take an eye break and the position of his screen is below eye level, which lead him to severe chronic dry eye. Because he is too busy in his work, doesn't have time to visit the mall or other establishment that have optical clinic and also he's not sure about the services offered by the clinic. How Lebron use the app, first he registered using his Facebook account, then he searched for the nearest optical clinic after that, the map appeared then the app pin-point all the nearest clinic. He viewed each clinic detail and the services offered by the certain clinic. After he chose the nearest clinic the he want to have appointment based on his schedule, he clicked on 'schedule now' then set the date/time. After the submitting the schedule details the app verify the schedule. Then the app notifies him within the day of his schedule. The app helps him to save his time and hassle-free for him in going to other places to find a nearest optical clinic and also it doesn't affect his work time.
